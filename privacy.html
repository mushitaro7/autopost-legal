Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 10, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Auto Post App ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our social media automation service.

2. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: When you connect your TikTok account, we receive your TikTok user ID and access tokens.
  • Content Data: We process the videos and descriptions you create for posting.
  • Usage Data: We collect information about how you use the app, including posting schedules and preferences.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To post content to your connected TikTok account on your behalf
  • To schedule and manage your social media posts
  • To improve our services and user experience

4. Data Storage and Security

Your access tokens are stored securely on your local device. We do not store your TikTok credentials on external servers. All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS.

5. Third-Party Services

We integrate with TikTok's API to provide posting functionality. Your use of TikTok is also governed by TikTok's own Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

6. Your Rights

You can disconnect your TikTok account at any time through the Settings page. Upon disconnection, we will delete your access tokens immediately.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@autopost.app
